CeloHT – User Guide

1. What is CeloHT

CeloHT is a community-based Web3 platform built on the Celo network, structured around three core pillars:

1. Education – financial and digital literacy


2. Agents – community agents who guide and support users


3. Reforestation – environmental and tree‑planting initiatives



⚠️ CeloHT is NOT a cryptocurrency, NOT a token, and NOT an ICO.

CeloHT uses Celo cUSD via Valora Wallet to enable simple, transparent transactions.



2. Requirements Before Getting Started

An Android or iOS smartphone

Internet connection

Valora Wallet installed

A small amount of cUSD on the Celo network




3. Installing Valora Wallet

1. Open Google Play Store or Apple App Store


2. Search for Valora Wallet


3. Install the app


4. Create a wallet


5. Securely store your recovery phrase



⚠️ Never share your recovery phrase with anyone.



4. Getting cUSD

You can obtain cUSD:

Through a CeloHT agent

Via a crypto exchange that supports the Celo network

From another Celo user


cUSD is used to:

Access educational activities

Pay for services

Support reforestation projects




5. Joining the CeloHT Ecosystem

1. Visit the official CeloHT platform (when available)


2. Connect your Valora Wallet


3. Confirm the connection


4. Your account becomes active on CeloHT





6. Using CeloHT by Pillar

6.1 Education

Access educational content

Learn about crypto, finance, and Web3

Participate in workshops and community sessions


Some content may require a cUSD contribution.




6.2 Agents

CeloHT agents:

Guide new users

Explain how the platform works

Assist with cUSD and Valora usage


⚠️ Agents are strictly prohibited from:

Requesting private keys

Promising profits or guaranteed returns




6.3 Reforestation

Contribute cUSD to tree‑planting initiatives

Track environmental actions

Access transparent reports on reforestation activities




7. User Security Best Practices

To protect yourself:

Never share private keys or recovery phrases

Do not send funds through unverified links

Use only official CeloHT communication channels

Always verify wallet addresses before transactions


CeloHT will never ask for:

Passwords

Recovery phrases
