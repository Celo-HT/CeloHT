# Press Kit — CeloHT

## Short Description
CeloHT is a Haitian-led initiative focused on education, digital financial inclusion through cUSD/Valora, and reforestation efforts aligned with Celo’s climate-positive mission.

## Key Facts
- **Founded:** 2022 update 2025 
- **Nature:** Community-driven, nonprofit  
- **Mission:** Expand financial access in Haiti using Celo blockchain tools while supporting environmental regeneration  
- **Core Pillars:** Education • Financial Inclusion • Tree Planting  
- **Contact:** celoht3@gmail.com  

## What CeloHT Does
- Provides structured education on Celo, cUSD, Valora, Web3 fundamentals, and digital financial skills adapted to Haiti  
- Helps communities adopt cUSD and Valora safely through practical guides, workflows, and real-use integration  
- Supports tree-planting initiatives aligned with Celo’s climate-positive ecosystem  
- Develops practical community programs that connect education, mobile payments, and environmental action across local groups  
- Creates clear pathways for Haitians to access digital tools, earn, participate, and contribute inside the Celo ecosystem
