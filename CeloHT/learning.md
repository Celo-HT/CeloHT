# Overview / Apèsi

:::tabs

@tab EN
CeloHT Learning Hub provides structured, professional education for users, developers, and entrepreneurs.  
**Goal:** Make CELO, cUSD, Valora, and CeloHT ecosystem accessible globally.

> **Pro Tip:** Always verify official contract addresses and wallets before sending funds.

@tab HT
Sant Aprantisaj CeloHT ofri edikasyon estriktire, pwofesyonèl pou itilizatè, devlopè, ak antreprenè.  
**Objektif:** Fè CELO, cUSD, Valora ak ekosistèm CeloHT aksesib mondyalman.

> **Konsèy:** Toujou verifye adrès kontra ak bous ofisyèl avan voye kripto.

:::
