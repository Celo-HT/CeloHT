## About CeloHT
CeloHT is a financial platform built on the Celo ecosystem, providing users with fast and secure access to the **cUSD stablecoin** and the mobile application **Valora**. Our goal is to simplify digital transactions for both local and international communities.

**Mission:** To deliver a transparent, simple, and secure digital financial experience for all users.  
**Vision:** To create an accessible, reliable, and internationally compliant financial ecosystem.

## FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

**Q1: What does CeloHT offer?**  
A: Access to cUSD, the Valora app, and a secure digital transaction platform.

**Q2: How can I create an account?**  
A: Download the Valora app and follow the registration steps. Once your account is created, you can use it on CeloHT.

**Q3: Are transactions free?**  
A: Some minimal fees may apply depending on the Celo network and partner services.

**Q4: How can I secure my account?**  
A: Use a strong password, enable two-factor authentication, and never share your private keys.

**Q5: What should I do if I lose access to my account?**  
A: Contact CeloHT support immediately and follow the account recovery procedures in Valora.
