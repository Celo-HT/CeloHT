# DECISION_LOG.md
## CeloHT 
 Governance Decision Log

### Purpose
This document records all major governance decisions to ensure transparency, accountability, and auditability.

### Decision Record Format
Each decision must include:
- Decision ID
- Title
- Date
- Proposal Reference
- Decision Body
- Voting Outcome
- Status
- Public Notes
