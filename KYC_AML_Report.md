# CeloHT KYC / AML Report
## 1. Organization Overview
**Organization Name:** CeloHT  
**Type:** Educational & financial facilitation initiative  
**Focus Areas:**  
- Financial literacy & crypto education  
- Agent network facilitation for cUSD transactions  
- Environmental impact through reforestation initiatives  
**Core Pillars:**  
1. Education  
2. Agents  
3. Reforestation  
**Platform Used for Transactions:** Valora wallet  
**Token Involvement:** cUSD only; CeloHT does not issue tokens or engage in ICOs/IDOs  

## 2. Regulatory & Compliance Position
**KYC/AML Policy:**  
- CeloHT does not act as a custodial service  
- CeloHT does not operate as a bank, MSB, or remittance service  
- CeloHT does not hold, manage, or intermediate user funds  
- CeloHT facilitates P2P cUSD transactions only via authorized agents  
**Risk Management:**  
- All participants undergo basic KYC verification via agents  
- Transactions monitored to detect suspicious activity in line with AML principles  
- CeloHT provides educational guidance on safe and compliant use of cUSD  

## 3. Customer Due Diligence (CDD)
### Identification

### Verification Process
- Document verification (manual or digital scans)

- Risk scoring for agents and participants based on volume and type of transactions  
### Record Retention
- KYC records retained securely in encrypted form for **5 years**  
- Minimal data collected to protect privacy and reduce liability  

## 4. Anti-Money Laundering Controls
**Transaction Monitoring:**  
- Agents trained to identify unusual transaction patterns  
- Large or frequent transfers flagged for review  
- P2P facilitation includes guidance on legal and compliant usage of cUSD  
**Suspicious Activity Reporting (SAR):**  
- Suspected fraudulent or illegal activity reported to local regulatory authorities  
- CeloHT maintains internal reporting system to ensure timely escalation  
**Training & Education:**  
- Continuous AML/KYC training for all agents and staff  
- Educational modules for users on recognizing and avoiding financial fraud  

## 5. Risk Assessment
| Risk Area                 | Assessment   | Mitigation Strategy |
|----------------------------|-------------|------------------|
| Regulatory non-compliance  | Low-Medium  | Strict internal KYC/AML policies, regular audits |
| Fraud / Money laundering   | Medium      | Agent training, transaction monitoring, SAR reporting |
| Data privacy               | Low         | Encryption, minimal data collection, access control |
| Reputation risk            | Low         | Transparent communication and reporting |

## 6. Summary & Recommendations
CeloHT operates as an educational and facilitation platform without holding or controlling user funds. Its KYC and AML procedures are aligned with international best practices, emphasizing participant verification through agents, transaction monitoring and reporting, and ongoing training and education.  
**Recommendations:**  
1. Periodic independent audit of KYC/AML procedures  
2. Update agent risk scoring quarterly based on transaction behavior  
3. Expand user education on compliance and financial safety
