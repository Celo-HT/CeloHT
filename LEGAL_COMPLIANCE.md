### 1. Scope
This document defines the legal, compliance, and regulatory framework governing CeloHT. CeloHT operates as a non-custodial, education-focused Web3 initiative built on the Celo blockchain. CeloHT does not provide financial, investment, or custodial services.

### 2. Terms of Service
All users must accept the Terms of Service prior to accessing any CeloHT platform or service. The Terms of Service define user responsibilities, acceptable use, limitation of liability, intellectual property rights, and disclaimers, including the absence of financial advice.

### 3. Privacy & Data Protection
CeloHT applies strict data-minimization principles. No unnecessary personal data is collected. No user data is sold or shared. CeloHT does not custody user funds or private keys. Any collected data is used solely for operational or educational purposes.

### 4. KYC / AML Policy
CeloHT does not require KYC or AML procedures for access to educational content, non-custodial tools, or community participation. KYC/AML applies only when legally required by jurisdictional law, institutional partnerships, payment providers, or grant frameworks. In such cases, compliance is delegated to certified third-party providers.

### 5. Regulatory Position
CeloHT does not issue securities, conduct ICOs, IDOs, or token sales, manage user funds, guarantee returns, or provide investment services. CeloHT operates strictly as a Web3 education and social-impact initiative.

### 6. Legal Structure
CeloHT is designed to operate under a legally compliant structure that enables separation between governance and operations, international compliance, and transparent accountability. Final jurisdiction and registration details will be publicly disclosed once finalized.

### 7. Risk Disclosure
Web3 regulations vary by jurisdiction and are subject to change. Users interact with CeloHT at their own risk and are responsible for understanding and complying with applicable local laws.

### 8. Updates
This document may be updated to reflect regulatory or legal changes. All updates are versioned and publicly documented.
