## Governance Model

CeloHT is governed by:
- A decentralized community
- A coordination committee
- Transparent decision-making

No single maintainer has unilateral control.

## Maintainer Responsibilities

- Review pull requests
- Enforce project standards
- Ensure compliance with:
  - Web3 best practices
  - Legal and non-token positioning
- Protect the integrity of the 3 pillars:
  - Education
  - Agent
  - Reforestation

## Becoming a Maintainer

Requirements:
- Proven contributions
- Community trust
- Technical competence
- Agreement with CeloHT principles

Approval:
- Community review
- Committee consensus

## Removal of Maintainers

A maintainer may be removed if:
- Inactive for extended periods
- Violates project principles
- Compromises security or transparency
