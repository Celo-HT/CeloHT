# CeloHT — Proof of Impact

## 1. Overview
CeloHT is a community-led Haitian initiative founded in 2025 with the mission to expand the adoption of Celo and cUSD across Haiti through education, access to digital money, and environmental restoration programs.  
All impact data referenced in this report is publicly available on the official CeloHT website.



## 2. Mission Statement
To empower the Haitian population with open, stable, and transparent financial tools built on the Celo blockchain, while strengthening education, financial inclusion, and environmental regeneration.



## 3. Core Impact Pillars

### 3.1 Education & Digital Literacy
- Production of public educational PDFs and guides in Haitian Creole.  
- Technical documentation for beginners and professionals.  
- Community workshops on blockchain, cUSD, Valora, and digital security.  
- Continuous publication of simplified training materials on the CeloHT website.

### 3.2 Financial Inclusion & cUSD Adoption
- Onboarding support for new Valora users.  
- Promotion of cUSD as a stable digital currency accessible to anyone with a smartphone.  
- Guidance for unbanked populations to access modern financial tools.  
- Public transparency on financial activity through CeloHT’s official platforms.

### 3.3 Environmental & Forestation Initiatives
- Promotion of tree-planting activities.  
- Community engagement in environmental preservation.  
- Alignment with Celo’s global regenerative finance vision.  
- Public documentation of environmental actions through the CeloHT site.

## 8. Risk & Mitigation

### 8.1 Identified Risks
- Limited digital literacy in underserved regions  
- Smartphone and internet access constraints  
- Security risks for new digital currency users  
- Environmental program logistics inside vulnerable areas

### 8.2 Mitigation Measures
- Educational materials in simple Creole  
- Hands-on training for first-time users  
- Security tutorials for safe cUSD & Valora usage  
- Public documentation of environmental processes
