# CeloHT About & Team

## About CeloHT

CeloHT is a community-centered blockchain initiative designed to expand access to digital payments, strengthen financial inclusion, and support environmental restoration.  
The project is structured around three core pillars:

- **Education**  
- **Agent Operations**  
- **Reforestation Efforts**

CeloHT leverages the Celo blockchain, cUSD, and the Valora mobile wallet to provide fast, low-cost, accessible financial tools for underserved communities.

The organization operates under a **community governance model**, where members can vote on governance decisions, propose improvements, and elect contributors who help reinforce operations, education, and local initiatives.

## Team Structure

CeloHT’s operational framework is built on four essential divisions:

### **Technical Division**
Responsible for:
- Smart contracts  
- Platform development  
- On-chain transparency tools  
- Security reviews and CI/CD automation  

### **Operations & Agent Network**
Manages:
- Agent onboarding and training  
- Cash-in / cash-out logistics  
- Merchant onboarding  
- Anti-fraud procedures  
- Field operations  

### **Education Division**
In charge of:
- Training programs  
- Learning modules (online and in-person)  
- Digital literacy initiatives  
- Community outreach  

### **Reforestation & Impact**
Coordinates:
- Environmental projects  
- Tree-planting initiatives  
- Impact reporting  
- Partnerships with local organizations  



## Governance & Multisig Treasury

CeloHT uses a **5-signer multisig wallet** to ensure transparency and shared responsibility.

**Signers include:**
- Johnny Dubic (Founder)  
- Technical Lead  
- Operations Lead  
- Education Lead  
- External Advisor or Auditor  

**Rule:** 3 of 5 signatures required for transactions.

All treasury activity is transparent and publicly verifiable on-chain.
