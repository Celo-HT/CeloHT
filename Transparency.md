Transparency Policy 
    
CeloHT

1. Purpose

This document defines the transparency standards of CeloHT. Its objective is to ensure trust, accountability, and clarity for the community, partners, contributors, and the public.

CeloHT operates under the principles of openness, verifiability, and ethical governance.



2. Project Nature

CeloHT is NOT a cryptocurrency token

CeloHT does NOT issue any native token

CeloHT is NOT an investment product


CeloHT is a Web3-enabled platform built on three fundamental pillars:

1. Education


2. Agent-based services


3. Reforestation



The platform leverages Celo blockchain infrastructure, cUSD, and Valora solely as tools for payments, incentives, and community operations.



3. Financial Transparency

CeloHT commits to clear financial disclosure:

All funds handled are denominated in cUSD or fiat equivalents

No speculative assets are issued or promoted

No hidden fundraising mechanisms


3.1 Use of Funds

Funds are allocated strictly to:

Educational programs

Agent network operations

Reforestation activities

Platform maintenance and development


3.2 Reporting

Periodic financial summaries may be published

Major expenses are documented

Community-impact metrics are shared publicly when applicable




4. Governance Transparency

CeloHT is founded by Johnny Dubic

Strategic decisions are guided by a decentralized governance committee

Community input is encouraged through open discussions and proposals


Governance documents are publicly available in this repository.



5. Technical Transparency

Core documentation is hosted publicly on GitHub

APIs, SDKs, and specifications are documented

No hidden smart contracts or undisclosed on-chain logic


If smart contracts are used:

They are open-source

They are auditable

Their purpose is clearly defined




6. Risk Disclosure

CeloHT clearly states:

Participation is voluntary

There are no guaranteed financial returns

Users are responsible for their own decisions


CeloHT does not provide financial, legal, or investment advice.



7. Compliance & Ethics

CeloHT aligns with:

KYC/AML principles where applicable

Ethical Web3 standards

Anti-fraud and anti-misrepresentation policies


No misleading claims, false promises, or deceptive marketing practices are permitted.




8. Communication Transparency

Official communications are published through verified channels

No private or unofficial fundraising is allowed

Any partnership or collaboration is publicly disclosed





9. Data Transparency & Privacy

Only essential data is collected

No sale of user data

Data usage policies are documented


Privacy and transparency are balanced according to international best practices.




10. Updates

This Transparency Policy may be updated as the project evolves.

All updates will be:

Versioned

Timestamped

Publicly accessible

For transparency-related inquiries:
