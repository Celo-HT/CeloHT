# Business Model

## An Important Clarification

CeloHT is **not an investment**. There's no equity, no promised return, and
no financial instrument sold as a wealth-building opportunity. This is a
business model for a **community-governed nonprofit initiative**, not a
commercial company.

## Funding Sources

### 1. Ecosystem grants

Applications to Celo Foundation grant programs and other funding aligned
with financial inclusion and environmental sustainability.

### 2. Strategic partnerships

Collaboration with organizations that fund or support specific projects (for
example, a dedicated grant for a reforestation cycle).

### 3. Community contributions

Voluntary donations and support through tools like GitHub Sponsors (see
[`.github/FUNDING.yml`](../.github/FUNDING.yml)).

## How Resources Get Used

- Developing and translating educational material
- Operational support for the agent network (training, materials)
- cUSD rewards for verified reforestation activity
- Technical infrastructure (website, documentation, workflows)

## Long-Term Sustainability

Our long-term goal is a model where the agent network becomes increasingly
self-sustaining through reasonable service fees, reducing dependence on
grants alone over time.

## Relationship to FreClean

FreClean, the founder's second initiative, is a cleaning services business
operating separately but sharing a vision around local entrepreneurship. The
long-term plan includes formalizing a partnership between the two
initiatives, where FreClean's traction serves as proof of execution for
CeloHT's funders.
