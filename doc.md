# CeloHT: Uniting the Haitian Community through Digital Finance and Sustainability

## Overview

CeloHT is a Web3 initiative designed to provide practical digital financial infrastructure, education, and environmental impact solutions to the Haitian community. The project combines global best practices from projects like Celo, Binance, and XRP while adapting fully to local needs.

CeloHT is **not speculative**; it delivers real value to its users through education, measurable environmental projects, and accessible digital payments via **cUSD** and the **Valora app**.

---

## Vision

CeloHT aims to build a financially inclusive, environmentally responsible, and digitally empowered Haitian community by focusing on **three pillars**:

### 1. Financial Education
- Provide accessible resources and training to understand digital and local currencies.
- Help community members make informed financial decisions.
- Support youth, women, and small business owners in navigating the digital economy.

### 2. Environmental Action
- Promote measurable reforestation and sustainable projects.
- Track impact with transparency, showing tangible results for the community.
- Encourage ecological responsibility as a community norm.

### 3. Accessible Digital Finance
- Enable secure, easy transactions via **cUSD** and **Valora**.
- Support local businesses and community projects.
- Facilitate participation in the digital economy, even for beginners.

---

## Community Inclusion & Transparency

CeloHT is built for everyone:
- Urban and rural residents
- Small business owners
- Youth and women
- Professionals and community leaders

**Transparency measures** include:
- Publicly available team information with roles and verifiable credentials (LinkedIn/GitHub).
- Open-source technical code repositories.
- Regular updates on projects and financial reports.
