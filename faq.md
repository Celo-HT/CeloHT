# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

**Is CeloHT a cryptocurrency or a token?**
No. CeloHT is a community-governed, open-source initiative that *uses* the
Celo ecosystem (CELO, cUSD) as infrastructure it isn't itself a token or a
blockchain.

**Can I invest in CeloHT?**
No, CeloHT doesn't offer any investment product. We're an education and
financial inclusion initiative, not an investment platform.

**Who's behind CeloHT?**
CeloHT was founded by Johnny Dubic, based in Léogâne, Haiti. See
[`maintainers.md`](maintainers.md) for the current list of maintainers.

## About the Technology

**Why Celo specifically?**
Celo is built for mobile-first access with low transaction fees, which fits
well in places where mobile data reaches further than traditional banking.

**Do I need to understand blockchain to participate?**
No. Our education modules are designed for people with zero technical
background.

**How do I become a CeloHT agent?**
