# Legal Status

## Nature of This Initiative

CeloHT is a **community-driven, open-source initiative**, not a regulated
financial institution, a bank, or a securities issuer. Code and content in
this repository are shared under the [Apache 2.0 License](../LICENSE).

## What CeloHT Is Not (Legal Clarification)

To avoid regulatory confusion, we state this explicitly. CeloHT is **not**:

- A blockchain or a Layer-1 network (we use Celo, which exists independently of us)
- A cryptocurrency, token, or memecoin that we issue
- An Initial Coin Offering (ICO), an Initial DEX Offering (IDO), or any form
  of securities offering
- An investment platform or a financial advisor
- A DeFi (decentralized finance) protocol
- A cryptocurrency exchange
- An NFT project
- A staking platform

## Official Legal Description

As used consistently across our documentation: **"A community-driven,
open-source initiative built on the Celo ecosystem."**

## Not Legal or Financial Advice

Content in this repository is provided for educational purposes only. It
does not constitute legal, tax, or investment advice. Users should consult a
qualified professional for decisions specific to their own situation.

## Jurisdiction

CeloHT operates primarily out of Haiti. Users in other jurisdictions are
responsible for verifying compliance with their local laws regarding the use
of cryptocurrency tools.
