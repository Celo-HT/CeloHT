# Logo Usage

## Official Logo

The official CeloHT logo is available at
[`assets/branding/celoht-logo-primary.png`](../assets/branding/celoht-logo-primary.png).

The logo depicts a stylized navy "C" with a rising gold arrow/graph element,
symbolizing growth and financial progress in the official brand colors
navy (`#0B1120`) and gold (`#F5C842`).

## Clear Space

Keep a minimum clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "C" in
every direction don't place text or graphic elements within that zone.

## Minimum Size

- **Digital**: don't go below 32×32 pixels for a favicon/icon; 120px wide
  for standard use
- **Print**: don't go below 15mm wide

## What's Allowed

- Using the logo as-is, unmodified, on a background with sufficient contrast
- Resizing it proportionally (no stretching or squeezing)
- Using a transparent-background version when integrating it into other material

## What's Prohibited

- Changing the logo's colors (except an official monochrome version, if one
  is published)
- Adding effects (extra shadows, outlines, filters) not present in the
  original file
- Stretching, rotating, or distorting the logo
- Combining the logo with another brand's mark without written approval

For a vector (SVG), monochrome, or other format, contact
