# Partnerships

## Who We're Looking to Partner With

### The Celo ecosystem

Grants, technical support, and visibility through official Celo channels
(the Celo Foundation, Celo Forum, community grant programs).

### Local Haitian organizations

Collaboration with NGOs, cooperatives, and community groups that already
have trust and presence in the areas we operate, particularly for education
and reforestation programs.

### Educational institutions

Partnerships with schools and training centers to fold our financial
literacy and Web3 material into programs that already exist.

### Environmental partners

Organizations already working on reforestation or sustainable agriculture in
Haiti, so we complement existing work instead of duplicating it.

## What We Bring to a Partnership

- Access to an engaged community and a growing agent network
- Training material that's already culturally and linguistically adapted
- Full transparency through the public documentation in this repository
- A track record of actually shipping content and executing (see
  [`history.md`](history.md))

## Starting a Conversation

Reach out at **partnerships@celoht.com** with a short description of your
organization and how you see a potential collaboration working.

See [`business-model.md`](business-model.md) to understand CeloHT's general
economic model before that first conversation.
