# Press Kit

## For Journalists and Content Creators

This document provides the essential facts for anyone writing about CeloHT.

## Key Facts

- **Name**: CeloHT
- **Founded**: Léogâne, Haiti, by Johnny Dubic
- **Nature**: A community-driven, open-source initiative built on the Celo
  ecosystem **not** a cryptocurrency, token, or investment platform
- **Three Pillars**: Education, Agent Network, Reforestation
- **Technology**: Celo, CELO, cUSD, and Valora-compatible wallet integration
- **Primary Language**: Haitian Creole, with documentation in English for
  the international community
- **Recognition**: Featured in a Celo Forum "Founders" post (March 2026)

## Potential Story Angles

- How a Haitian-founded initiative approaches financial inclusion through
  mobile-first blockchain infrastructure
- The role of language-appropriate education in adopting financial technology
- Combining financial inclusion with environmental sustainability in a
  single model
- A community governance model for Caribbean open-source initiatives
