# Values

## 1. Language access comes before everything else

Financial and technical knowledge should be available in the language people
actually use every day. That's why **Haitian Creole is the primary language**
for our educational material — not a translation added on afterward.

## 2. Radical transparency

Governance, financial decisions, and internal processes should be documented
publicly. This repository is itself an expression of that value.

## 3. Community first

Decisions should serve the community, not the personal interests of any one
individual. We build *with* the community, not just *for* it.

## 4. Technology as a tool, not the point

Blockchain, cUSD, and Valora are a means to an end — financial inclusion —
not the end itself. We never promote the technology for its own sake.

## 5. Environmental sustainability

Economic growth and environmental health aren't competing goals. We refuse
to treat them that way.

## 6. Financial honesty

We never present CeloHT as an investment, a wealth-building opportunity, or
anything that guarantees a return. We're direct about the risks that come
with any crypto tool, and we don't give personal financial advice.

## 7. We keep learning

The crypto ecosystem, Web3 technology, and community needs all change. We
stay flexible and adjust our approach based on what actually works, not on
what sounded good on paper.
